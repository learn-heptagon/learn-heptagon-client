4.4 Traffic Light

  1. Walk is never on when cars are allowed
  2. Red and Green are never on at the same time
  3. At all times one of the tree colored lights is on
  4. Walk is on only if Red is
  5. Walk and DontWalk are mutually exclusive
  6. Red cannot immediately follow Green
  7. Walk is not on if cars were allowed in the previous step
  8. Cars are not allowed if Walk was on in the previous step
  9. Yellow is on for at most one step
  10. Red changes directly to green
